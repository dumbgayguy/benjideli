Our STORY

It started with a meat slicer, a family recipe, and a stubborn belief that good things take time.

When Benji opened these doors in 1963, he had one rule: do it right or don't do it at all. That meant slicing every sandwich by hand. Sourcing only Vienna Beef‚Äîno exceptions.

Six decades later, not much has changed. The same recipes. The same commitment to piling it high and making it right. We've watched Milwaukee grow up around us, fed generations of families, and become part of the fabric of this city.

Some things are worth doing the old way.
